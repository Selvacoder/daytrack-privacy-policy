Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

At Day Track, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: Day Track is designed as a fully local productivity tool. We do not require account registration, and we do not collect or store any of your personal data, habits, tasks, diet entries, or financial logs on remote servers. All your personal productivity data is stored securely and solely on your local device.

Advertisement Data: Our app integrates with the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain diagnostic or device identifiers (like advertising IDs, device model, screen size, language) to serve relevant, personalized or non-personalized ads, and perform analytics.

2. How We Use Information

  • Your local productivity data is used solely within the app to help you track habits, manage tasks, log diet, track finances, and view reports.
  • Ad-related identifiers are used by Google AdMob to deliver ads, prevent ad fraud, and provide analytics on ad performance.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob for advertisement services. Google's advertising systems collect data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review on Google's Partner Privacy Policy page.

4. Data Retention

Since all of your productivity data is stored locally on your device, it remains on your device until you manually clear it, uninstall the application, or manually delete it.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app settings.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at your support email address.
